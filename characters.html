Rikka

Rikka is the protagonist of the game. She is a young woman who is preparing to leave her old apartment with lingering memories that she cannot clearly remember. But as she packs her things, she discovers objects that uncover fragments of a hidden tragedy and a forgotten person she was never meant to leave behind.

The Entity

The Entity is a grotesque manifestation of Rikka's guilt and trauma, and it relentlessly pursues her throughout the nightmare. The objective of the nightmare is to survive and escape from the Entity while navigating through the eerie and unsettling environment.

Ren

Ren is a mysterious figure that appears in Rikka's nightmares. He is her lover and the person she is trying to remember throughout the game.

Ren is a kind and gentle soul who deeply cares for Rikka but is very career driven which sometimes conflicts with his desire to be close to her.
