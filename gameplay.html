Load State

Memoir has an automatic save feature that saves after completing each day's tasks. The players are able to load their saved games from the main menu or during gameplay and can only load saves from daytime.

Once the day has been reached, it will be available for loading.

The Apartment

The setting of the game. Each day, the apartment slowly deteriorates as the story progresses. Showing how the environment slowly reveals its true nature as Rikka discovers more about her past and the truth she has been avoiding.

Day and Night Cycle

Daytime

Rikka is tasked with packing up her belongings during the day on a designated area.

This is when the game saves and where the player loads their progress.

Nighttime

During the night, Rikka discovers an item after packing up. After a long day, her objective is now to sleep.

Nightmare

Rikka's nightmare sequence is a 2D side-scrolling level where she must run away from The Entity. The Entity is a grotesque manifestation of Rikka's guilt and trauma, and it relentlessly pursues her throughout the nightmare. The objective of the nightmare is to survive and escape from the Entity while navigating through the eerie and unsettling environment.
